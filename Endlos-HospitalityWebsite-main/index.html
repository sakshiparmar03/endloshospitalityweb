Project Management Overview

From initial layout planning to final installation, this process ensures efficient design, brand approval, quality production, and timely delivery, resulting in high-quality, customized spaces tailored to client needs.

1. Layout & Measurements

Clients provide site measurements, based on which we design a functional and efficient layout tailored to the space.

2. 3D Visualization and Renders

We craft high-quality 3D visualizations and renders that provide clients with a clear and realistic preview of their space.

3. Keyline Drawing

Detailed keyline drawings are prepared, showing exact dimensions, materials, and specifications to guide the production team.

4. Brand Approval and Agreement Signing

Final designs are submitted for brand or client approval, followed by the signing of agreements to begin production.

5. Production

Our manufacturing team starts production, ensuring strict quality control and adherence to the approved designs and specifications.

6. Dispatch , Delivery and Installation

Completed products are carefully packed, dispatched to the project site, and installed efficiently to ensure a smooth handover.

What types of furniture do you manufacture?

We manufacture hospitality furniture and case goods for hotel brands, including beds and headboards, desks, wardrobes, and seating for guest rooms, lobbies, dining areas, and more.

Can you design custom furniture collections for our hotel brand?

Absolutely, our team designs custom furniture based on your hotel brand’s needs. We also create collections for independent hotels looking for something specific.

Do you have experience working with hotel chains?

Over the years, we’ve partnered with several hotel chains across the US, providing quality furniture designed and built for their hotels' honor and guest spaces.

What’s your typical production and delivery timeline?

As soon as the design is approved, we begin right away. Production and dispatch are completed within 4 to 6 weeks.

Where is your manufacturing facility located?

We manufacture all our furniture in Gujarat, near the Ahmedabad region.

How do you handle shipping and logistics to the USA?
